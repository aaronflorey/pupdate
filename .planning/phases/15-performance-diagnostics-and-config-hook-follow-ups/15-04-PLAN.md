# Plan 15-04 - Add benchmarks and latency guardrails

## Objective

Protect the low-latency shell-hook promise by adding focused benchmarks and CI guardrails around the highest-value hot paths.

## Scope

- Target the most important latency-sensitive paths rather than broad benchmark coverage.
- Keep CI guardrails practical and stable enough for repeatable enforcement.
- Avoid premature optimization outside the measured hot paths.

## Tasks

1. Identify the hot-path operations whose latency most directly affects shell navigation responsiveness.
2. Add focused benchmarks and define acceptable regression signals for local and CI use.
3. Integrate the smallest reliable CI or validation guardrail that catches meaningful latency drift.

## Verification

- Benchmark output captured for the targeted hot paths.
- CI or validation configuration review confirming the guardrail is exercised automatically.

## Done When

- The repo has explicit performance measurements for the most latency-sensitive paths.
- Regressions in those paths have an automated detection mechanism.
